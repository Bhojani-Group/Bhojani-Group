Ragi (finger millet)
Rs. 100 per kg
Ragi, also known as finger millet, is a highly nutritious grain that is a staple food in many parts of India and Africa. It is rich in essential nutrients such as calcium, iron, and dietary fiber, making it beneficial for bone health, blood sugar regulation, and digestion. Ragi is gluten-free and packed with antioxidants, supporting overall health and well-being. Its versatility in cooking, from porridge to baked goods, makes it a popular choice for those seeking a nutritious addition to their diet.
